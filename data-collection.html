Data Collection Practices

At AI Hub, we prioritize user privacy and are committed to transparency regarding the data we collect.

What Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as name, email address, etc., when you interact with our website (e.g., sign-up forms, contact forms).
  • Usage Data: We collect information on how users interact with our site, such as pages visited, time spent, and clicks.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance user experience, track usage patterns, and store user preferences.
  • Third-party Services: Some data may be collected by third-party services like Firebase, Google Analytics, etc. These services help us improve our platform and understand user behavior.

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To improve the functionality and user experience of our website.
  • To send notifications or updates about our services, with your consent.
  • To analyze website traffic and enhance performance using services like Google Analytics and Firebase.

How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request the correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. However, we may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our services and improving the user experience. These third parties are obligated to keep your data confidential.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this data collection policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the most recent update will be shown at the top of this page.

If you have any questions about our data collection practices, please contact us.
